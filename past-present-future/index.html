Past Present Future

05 June 2016 on Articles

You live in present right? What is the length of present? One second?
No. Less than that.

Past is in our memory and the future is in the expectation and in the
results of what we did and do in past and present. With all these we live
in a single (Physically dimensionless) moment which is called
the reality of our existence.

We say past present future. But our existence in the present stands as the divider. Existence is the reality that we must accept.

We are alive in a single moment.
Like we are always in a compartment.
Changes.
Unidirectional trip.
Two doors.
One transparent and is prohibited to pass.
Another one is opened but not possible to see which is behind.
So many false drawings on the door.
But all we know the destiny.
Our hope is to reach the phase of the Clarity.
Our destiny is a meeting.
A Holy Meeting.
This is what we are waiting for.

Anwarul Ghani
