Physics Regular course for HSC

This course contains two classes per week of 1.5 hours duration each. It includes lectures, lab opportunities, practice and exams. Students must not come with the traditional idea of getting good GPA other than having good knowledge on the specific subject. We give priority to student’s curiosity and basic conception. Our goal is to make them self-inspired to reach the required level of understanding.
