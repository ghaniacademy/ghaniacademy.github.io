Talking Parrot

05 June 2016 on Articles

Think aobut the parrot!
When it was singing its words the owner found it as usless noise and taught it his meaningful words. Once it learns to utter the words of its owner...
he says, 'O my parrot can talk now!'
But the fact is, it has forgotten to speak its own!
Its soul always cry its own.
We are not parrots. We dont want to be. Our communication with wordily communicates must not be like the above allegory.
Dont make me talk like you and i wont like you to talk like me. As we feel unlike so word helps a little. There is a communication of silent prayer that makes unlike like.

Anwarul Ghani
