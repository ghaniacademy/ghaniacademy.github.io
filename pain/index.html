Pain

05 June 2016 on Articles

It is not like what I want, it is what I see.
My peace is if I can accept it.
And pain is if I can't.
And mostly I can.
When I was unable to, there was a pain.
But now the pain is like a friend.
It helps me to understand that I am not understanding.

Anwarul Ghani
