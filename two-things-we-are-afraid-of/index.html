Two things we are afraid of


05 June 2016 on Articles

A sensible human being is afraid of two things. One is death and another is dishonor. Death being an event, which is sure to come anytime in life, can be acceptable and many can be free from its fear. And few can totally accept it as the better conclusion and get prepared to welcome it anytime.

Also it is possible to be free from the willing to gain honor from the society.

Human not afraid of death or dishonor is of the extreme kind or the ideal kind. The worst character human being is also free from the fear of death and also he may not care the question of honor. He wants power and he may enjoy torture.

A human of Ideal is not also afraid of death and dishonor while it is for the sake of humanity and Love. He is in Love whereas the worst one is not.

Anwarul Ghani
