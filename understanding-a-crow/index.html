Understanding a crow

I am a crow and I have only one sound 'kraa'. All I say are by repeating this 'kraa'. When your sound resembles to mine it draws my attention and I go near you. It is not for I am fool rather it is for I have the love for the sound as it is meaningful to me. You may not understand the meaning but while you utter it, it sounds like my Love is calling me.

Without being a proper listener you can never know what is said in a say. If you call a crow like the sound it makes, the crow will reply. But you not being a crow or the one to understand it's language will never know what was in his sound 'kraa kraa'.
One thing you must understand that you haven't understood.
