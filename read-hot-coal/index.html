Read hot coal

Day will come when it will be so difficult to keep faith that it will be just like holding a red hot coal in your hand.
Yes..now we see the days.. no doubt.
But once if you can hold it and have the taste of the pain.. you will even try to burn your heart with its fire.

Trials of composing Islam in different fashions are available in this so called civilized society. But Islam is not to be composed. You just hold the rope of Islam. It exists in its divineness. We cant imagine its amazing beauty unless we are in the reality of its practicing.
Save muslims..not Islam. You are the first Muslim to be saved. Get out form the illusive attraction of this civilization. The red hot coal can help you to burn your wishes.
May Allah help us.
