Variation of thought

Variation of thoughts is not any problem if there is honesty and tolerance. Man thinks on the context of circumstances. It is rarely possible to think generally to fit the idea with many variables. It is a natural limitation of man and we have to accept it. Our honesty can help integrity, tolerance can help opening up the mind to let positive and constructive ideas in... We deserve to have the quality to make communication with others... Possibly one of the most important ways to the path of knowledge is the proper communication with human thought. ..
