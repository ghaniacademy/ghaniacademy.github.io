Truth and beauty

05 June 2016 on Articles

Truth never mixes with false. But in our illusion we see them together in a composition. Sometimes this composition looks like a beauty. This is a description of the fact of illusion. If you search for truth only, you will find Beauty as well. This searching of truth may cause you to suffer from pain, besides it gives you the satisfaction to hold on with the suffering. There will be no objection.
But in searching of beauty illusion may come with a nice decorated deceit depending on your expectations. Remember, beauty allures you but truth protects you from the illusion of beauty. Beauty is seeking for your friendship. But truth is always your friend despite you do not love it. So, You be the lover of truth, the friend of truth, only then you can take the proper care of beauty.

Anwarul Ghani
