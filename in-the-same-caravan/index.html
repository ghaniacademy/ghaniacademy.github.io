In the same Caravan

Human life is a path of purification. Path of purification is the path of knowledge. One who is a traveler and knows his path can attain the destiny and a one who is a traveler but not knowing his path also attains. It happens when they are in the same caravan. What is the difference here?

Try to think that we all are in the same caravan of purification. But the stages are not coming to all of us with the same pleasure and troubles. Let us share the pleasure and let us help each other during the troubles.

Do you think that being a reader of books and having skills of writing expressions you are in the path of knowledge but not they are who are not in touch? It is true that you can't ensure the process for all. Actually this process is not necessary for all but how can you imagine that the One Who made it for all made it so that you the people are to taste it only? One whom you are ignoring is not ignored. One whom you honor may not be honored. Love is the connection. It is your sincere desire that can communicate.
