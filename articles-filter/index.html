Filter


05 June 2016 on Articles

Communication process... it's a sort of filter.
It should only allow to pass the good things.
We have to clean the filter time to time.
We can do it also and it is by the repentance to the Almighty Creator.

Clean your filter.
Don't let it to be just a way out.
If the particles are fine and perfect then the filter is not to be cleaned much.
It just becomes a formality.
And in that stage you may not feel the existence of the filter.

Yes.. This world around you is a formality.
But the real fact is not known unless you can communicate properly.
Try to understand the Love (the Filter) that doesn't allow the bad things in the communication.

Anwarul Ghani
