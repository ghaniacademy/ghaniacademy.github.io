O Lovers

When Love appears the reason disappears.
And once that the reason is gone it is of no use.
The Reason is one of the doors but Love is the Palace.
O lovers you are inside a palace.
Don't look back to the door. Let the door be closed.
Be trapped in love. See the beauty inside it. Do you see the palace destroyed? Listen to the cry of love from each and every brick of the palace. Why do you stop here? You have the love to reconstruct the palace. Don't worry!

All the lovers are sensitive and no lover is blind. No way, because love itself is a light. If you have the light of love no light can ever let you see things with more clarity. If you are truly in Love of a creation how is it possible to be unaware of The Creator?

Love is the ultimate phase of knowing. If the identity of your beloved is the creation of The Loving Creator then you be right at a stage before loving The Creator. But remember, you are in Love and you are the beloved. From your beloved for what you seek?

You are the fruit of love. You have the seed inside. Keep the seed with care because you are a conveyer of love.
