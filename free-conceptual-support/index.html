Free conceptual support


06 June 2016 on Curriculum

Ghani Academy offers a free opportunity for the learners to discuss their conceptual problems. In this weekly program on each Tuesday (3.00 pm t0 5.00 pm) and Friday(10.00 am to 12.00) students of SSC/HSC, O level and A level may come and discuss their problems related to science and anything of their curiosity related to scientific,philosophical or theological knowledge. Actually it is an open door. Ghani Academy welcomes anyone to come and share their views and ideas. It is appreciated that the person who wishes to come will make a pre-appointment.

Anwarul Ghani
