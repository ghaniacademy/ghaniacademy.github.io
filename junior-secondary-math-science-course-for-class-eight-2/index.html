Junior Secondary Math-Science Course (for class eight)

Students who are interested to study in science group to the next (SSC) level can get admitted for this course. This is a four month duration program and students will get three lectures weekly. In the three lectures we will try to make them introduced with three major subjects of pure science namely Physics Chemistry and Mathematics. Many of the students are not very fond of mathematics and science. But due to the social demand of science they take it as some boring and necessary subject. In our four months course we try to make an interesting introduction with scientific study. It helps them to better understand and enjoy the later coming up stages of upper level science study. The admission session of this course starts from January 01 and continues till the end of April.
