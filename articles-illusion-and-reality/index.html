Illusion and Reality


05 June 2016 on Articles

Someone said, 'It's illusion I want to create.'

The Replier : But the fact is.. Illusion presents in a reality.
We create nothing.
Our sense that 'we create' is an illusion.
Whatever false we say thinking as if it is true... is the illusion.
And this is the reality in which
fake things are alive on earth.

Illusion is the result of misunderstanding.

Now the question arises.

When will you understand that you are not in the illusion?
When you understand that you are in illusion
it means that you are not totally in the illusion.
In the reality, you will just be in it and will feel it.
Clarity is such a phase that cares no description
because it is such a vision that makes you see things, realize and enjoy. All the questions disappear in answers just like when you enter the palace the doors disappear one by one.

Anwarul Ghani
